## üéÆ How Each Lesson Works

### Step 1: Overview
- Read the lesson description
- See what you'll learn
- Click "Start Lesson" to begin

### Step 2: Study with Flashcards
- **How to use**: Click any flashcard to flip it
- **View**: Question on front, answer on back
- **Progress**: Study all 4 cards in the lesson
- **Continue**: Button appears only after studying all cards
- **Tip**: Read carefully and understand before moving on

### Step 3: Practice Quiz
- **Format**: 2 multiple choice questions per lesson
- **Answering**: Select an option, then click "Submit Answer"
- **Feedback**: See correct/incorrect instantly with explanation
- **Navigation**: Use the question numbers at bottom to skip around
- **Requirement**: Must answer all questions to finish

### Step 4: View Results
- **Score**: See your percentage and XP earned
- **Passing**: Score 70% or higher to complete the lesson
- **If Passed**: 
  - Lesson marked as completed ‚úì
  - Next lesson automatically unlocks
  - XP added to your total
- **If Failed**: 
  - Retry option to redo the quiz
  - Return to study flashcards if needed

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I skip lessons?**
A: No, lessons must be completed sequentially. This ensures you build strong foundational knowledge.

**Q: What score do I need to pass?**
A: You need 70% or higher. This means getting at least 2 out of 2 questions correct (or adjusted for more questions).

**Q: Can I retry a lesson?**
A: Yes! If you don't pass the quiz, you can immediately retry. You can also go back and review flashcards.

**Q: Do I lose XP if I retry?**
A: No, XP is only awarded once per lesson when you first pass it.

**Q: Can I go back to previous lessons?**
A: Yes, you can revisit any completed lesson to review the content.

**Q: How long does each lesson take?**
A: Usually 10-15 minutes (5 min flashcards + 5-10 min quiz).

**Q: Are the lessons mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes! All lessons work great on phones, tablets, and desktops.
